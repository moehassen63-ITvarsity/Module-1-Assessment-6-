Cupboard

A beautiful handcrafted cupboard that has room for hanged as well as coloded clothes.

The cupboard has 2 doors for hanging clothes, 2 drawers for loose clothes and 2 bottom doors for folded clothes.

Its finished in a beautiful dark oak wood and varnished for extra protection. The cupboard has oak handcrafted handles which are finished in a light paint coating.

The cupboard takes 2 weeks to build and is build by one of our major partners

With dimensions of 6ft tall and 1 meter wide the cupboard is well suited for any home.
